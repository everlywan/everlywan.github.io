Athletics

Bolder Boulder 10K

The Bolder Boulder is an annual 10-kilometer run in Boulder, Colorado. The 2007 race featured over 54,000 runners, making it the second largest largest 10k race in the US and the fifth largest road race in the world.

Dragon Boat Race

One of my favorite cultural events is the annual Dragon Boat Race, which I’ve participated in and enjoyed deeply. Dragon boat racing is more than just a sport—it’s a vibrant tradition rooted in the Chinese Dragon Boat Festival, or Duanwu Jie. This festival honors the ancient poet Qu Yuan and is celebrated by racing long, narrow boats in rhythmic unison to the beat of drums. I love the energy of the race, the teamwork it demands, and the cultural pride it inspires. Paddling together with others, feeling the water rush by, and hearing the crowd cheer—it’s an unforgettable experience that connects me not only to my heritage, but also to a larger community. For me, the dragon boat race isn’t just about winning—it’s about spirit, tradition, and joy.
